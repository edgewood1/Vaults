

1. **[[Stand-alone]]**: Tools that operate independently, accessed directly via their website or app. Often general-purpose or handle common specialized tasks.
    
    - _Examples_:
        - **Chatbots:** ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, Perplexity
        - **Image Generation:** Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion
        - **Writing/Productivity:** Jasper, Rytr, Notion (can be stand-alone or built-in)
        - **Audio/Video:** ElevenLabs (voice), Descript (video/audio editing)
        - **Design:** Canva (also has built-in features)
        - **Translation:** DeepL
2. **[[Built-in AI Enhancements]]**: AI features integrated directly _within_ other software platforms, enhancing their existing functionality.
    
    - _Examples_:
        - **Google Workspace:** Gemini features in Docs, Sheets, Gmail (e.g., writing help, summaries).
        - **Microsoft 365:** Copilot features in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams (e.g., drafting, data analysis, meeting summaries).
        - **Notion:** Notion AI for summarizing, action items, drafting within notes.
        - **Adobe Creative Cloud:** Firefly/Sensei features for image generation/editing within Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.
        - **Customer Relationship Management (CRM):** AI features within platforms like Salesforce for predicting sales trends or automating customer service tasks.
3. **[[Custom AI Solution]]**: AI systems specifically designed and developed to solve a unique problem or automate a specific process for a particular organization. These are not off-the-shelf products.
    
    - _Examples_:
        - A retail company's unique AI model predicting local demand for specific inventory items.
        - A bank's proprietary fraud detection system trained on its specific transaction patterns.
        - A factory's AI system for predictive maintenance tailored to its unique machinery.
        - A custom internal chatbot trained _only_ on a company's private knowledge base to answer employee questions.
        - AI used by a logistics company (like UPS's DeliveryDefense) to generate delivery confidence scores based on their internal data.
        - A healthcare system's AI tool analyzing patient data (following privacy regulations) to assist doctors with specific diagnostic pathways.
