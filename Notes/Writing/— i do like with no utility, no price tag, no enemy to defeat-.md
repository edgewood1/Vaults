 Necessary Warning
Molecular work is not without risk. The goal is not to dissolve all structure—pure structurelessness leads to disintegration, not liberation. We seek what Deleuze calls a “controlled deterritorialization,” a line of flight that creates new possibilities rather than mere chaos.
The fascist impulse also works at the molecular level, using fear and paranoia to contract connection and intensify separation. Our response is not to retreat to molar rigidity, but to work molecularly in the opposite direction—expanding connection, building a reality that includes rather than excludes
