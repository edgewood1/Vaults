# meta / epistem

**part one**

metaphysical / ontological - what exists? 

ideas and the minds the have them (subjective idealism)
materialism - matter

epistemological - what can we know? "What is knowledge?", "How is knowledge acquired?", and "What do people know?"

trancendental / critical idealism of kant - we can only know our “modes of representation” of  the existence of things”, , above all space and time, are not informed / created by the things themselves but by  features of our own minds.  

REALISM - we can know the things themselves. 

kinds of knowledge - a priori knowledge is possible independently of any experience and requires only use of reason rationalism) 

aposteriori - possible only fafter certain sense expeirence (empiricism)
