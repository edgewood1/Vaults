# Topics


1. Ask about their background: Ask where they're from, what they do, and how long they've been with their company.
2. Hobbies and Interests: Ask about their hobbies or interests outside of work, and share some of your own.
3. Favorite books/movies/TV shows: Ask about their favorite books, movies, or TV shows, and share your own recommendations.
4. Travel: Ask about their favorite travel destinations and if they have any upcoming trips planned.
5. Food: Ask about their favorite foods and restaurants, and share your own recommendations.
6. Sports: Ask if they are into any sports, teams, or competitions.
7. Current events: Ask if they've been following any recent news stories and share your own thoughts.
8. Technology: Ask about their favorite gadgets, apps, and tech-related topics
