# In Durham, NC, city garbage carts must be at the curb by 5 a.m. on…

In Durham, NC, city garbage carts must be at the curb by 5 a.m. on the scheduled day, with lids closed and placed at least 3 feet away from obstacles, other carts, mailboxes, or trees. Carts must be removed within 24 hours of pickup. One bag of overflow garbage is allowed on top of the cart. 
**Key Trash Pickup Rules & Policies**
* **Placement:** Place carts within 3 feet of the curb, ensuring they do not block sidewalks or driveways.
* **Time:** All items must be at the curb by 5 a.m. on the scheduled collection day.
* **Overflow:** One bag of extra garbage is permitted, but it must be placed on top of the green garbage cart.
* **Prohibited Items:** Do not place dirt, rocks, construction debris, concrete, hot ashes, hazardous liquids, paint cans, tires, or metal items in the regular garbage cart.
* **Cart Removal:** Carts must be removed from the curb within 24 hours of pickup.
* **Yard Waste:** Requires a separate brown cart (or up to 10 paper bags) and must be out by 6 a.m..
