# Zine - big bang


CMB is landmark evidence of the [Big Bang](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Bang) [theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_theory) for the origin of the universe. In the Big Bang [cosmological models](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cosmological_model), during the earliest periods, the universe was filled with an [opaque](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Opacity_(optics)) fog of dense, hot [plasma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plasma_(physics)) of [sub-atomic particles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sub-atomic_particle). As the universe expanded, this plasma cooled to the point where protons and electrons combined to form neutral atoms of mostly hydrogen.
