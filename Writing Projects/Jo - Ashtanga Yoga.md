* Slot arms through legs / wrap arms around legs and clap.

Kukku-Tasana
* Lotus
* Hand on floor side of hips
* Lift lotus off ground
* Hold for 5 breaths

Baddha Kona-sana 
* sit with soles of feet touching
* Knees bent
* Stamp down thigh to ground
* Head to ground as far away from feet.



Baddha kon-asana B
* same but 
* Head to feet
* Spine curved


=============

Upavistha Kona-sana
* sit with feet 120 degrees apart
* Touch toes
* Head far forward to ground



Upa-vishta Kona-asna B
* lift feet off the ground
* 45 degree angle
* Hold balance


=======================

Supta kon-asana
* lay down
* Feet over head to ground
* Feet 120 degrees apart
* Touch toes - breath
* Flip up to upa-vishta konasana B for a few sec.
* Upa-vishta konasana A
