# Conditional Love

What she sees between us is what she assumes is at the basis of all her life.

Sex, touch, kindness, sharing, conversation, connection — any form of these as bargaining chips (conditional vs unconditional love).

Her natural tendencies as a child resist this but are eventually overwhelmed.

The fact that she experiences some of these things at specific intervals (bedtime), and that at any moment they might be threatened by violence of speech or physicality.

The fact that one can become so obsessed about trivialities that it prevents us from seeing the larger picture, the creative context — and causes us to self-isolate even when surrounded by others.
