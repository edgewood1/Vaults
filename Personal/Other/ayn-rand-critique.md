# Ayn Rand Critique

People who feel they can do whatever they want — their deeds are good despite proof of the contrary. Those who critique them impose unjust rules meant to bloat government and impede progress. They're good because they love God, yet where is New Testament precedent for their deeds? They might point to the Kings, but these should be read in light of the New Testament.

Ayn Rand: if everyone suited themselves we'd live in a great place. Yet a middle-class family might have a child who goes through life unscathed, while a poor family might have a child who suffers due to laws that benefit the middle class.

*Example: Rep. Ronny Jackson — sexual comments, alcohol and Ambien use while serving as White House physician (Pentagon watchdog report).*
