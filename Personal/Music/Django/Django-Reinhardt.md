## Swing Gitane (Swing Gitan)

**Key:** G minor · rel: Bb major · 16 bars AA + 2 endings · Anon / trad Gypsy jazz

**Scales:**
- G natural minor: G A Bb C D Eb F
- G harmonic minor: G A Bb C D Eb F# (over A7/D7)
- Gm69 pentatonic: G A Bb D E (soloing)

**Chords:**
```
Gm6  A7  D7  A7
Gm6  A7  D7  Gm6
Ab6  Ab6  Gm6  Gm6
```
Ab6 = ♭II 'wild card' — half-step above Gm, creates tension

**Form (scale degrees):**
```
1 – 1 – 2 – 2 / 5 – 5 – 1 – 1 / b2 – b2 – 1 – 1
[End A]  2–2–4–4
[End B]  2–4–1–4
```

**Melody (head):**
```
D – Db – D – [Bb  A  A  G]
D – Db – D – Bb
D – Db – C – D – A
Db – C – Db – A / Db – C – B (resolve)
```

Solo: Gm arpeg over Gm6, A7b9 penta over A7, Ab7 arpeg over Ab6.

**Lick:**
```
D C D Bb*
D C Bb(l) C > A*
C B C > A*
C B A B > G
GGABb > F#
EEDDCD
```

**Scale:** G Harmonic Minor — Formula: 1 2 b3 4 5 b6 7 · Secondary: 5th mode of primary

## Limehouse Blues

**Key:** G major · rel: E minor · 32 bars AABA · Philip Braham, 1921

**Scales:**
- G major: G A B C D E F#
- G mixolydian: G A B C D E F (over dom chords)
- E minor: E F# G A B C D

**Chord structure (scale degrees):**
```
A:  4 … 4 … / 2 … 2 …
    6  6  3  6 / 2  2  5  4#
B:  6  6  2  2 – 4  5  3  1
```
Starts on C7 (IV7). Both A sections open C7 → A7.

**Melody:**
```
C7)  E → F# → G → E  |  F# → G
A7)  (repeat same phrase)
[End A-1]  Em7) D→E→F# | D→E→F#  (tension, unresolved D7)
[End A-2]  A7→A7→D7→C# / Cm→D7→Em7→Em7  (resolve to G)
```

## Sweet Georgia Brown

**Key:** G major · also F or D in Real Book · 32 bars · Bernie / Pinkard, 1925

**Scales:**
- G major: G A B C D E F#
- G minor pentatonic: G Bb C D F (safe over all changes)

**Chords (circle of 5ths):**
```

D7 – D7 – D7 – D7   (4 bars)
G  – G  – G  – G    (4 bars)
VI7 → II7 → V7 → I  (repeat / varied last 16 bars)
```

**Melody (head, in G):**
