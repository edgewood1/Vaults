## Pentatonic

**Major pentatonic:** 1 2 3 5 6

Intervals from each degree:
- 1 → major 2 → 3
- 2 → major 2 → 4
- 3 → min 3
- 5 → maj 2
- 6 → min 3 → 1

**Minor pentatonic:** 1 b3 4 5 b7

**Blues:** soloing principles, licks, rhythms (Guitar 6)

**Semitones:** 3–4 ; 7–1

**Balance:** 1 2 3 (2 steps) → ½ step → 1 step ; 5 6 7 (2 steps) → ½ → 1 step
