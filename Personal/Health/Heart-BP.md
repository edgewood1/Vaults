## Supine hypertension

When BP rises lying down but drops on sitting/standing: this pattern can indicate autonomic dysfunction, baroreceptor issues, or positional fluid redistribution. Not typical — worth discussing with cardiologist.

## Cardiologist tests available (beyond normal EKG)

Even with a normal EKG, a cardiologist can investigate chest pain and heart tension via: stress tests, echocardiogram, Holter monitor, coronary CT angiography. Normal EKG does not rule out underlying cardiac issues.
