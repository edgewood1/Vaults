Leaky stool
Some herbs that may help with leaky stool include:
* **Triphala**: An Ayurvedic blend of three plants that may help heal the gut lining, improve nutrient absorption, and relieve gastrointestinal distress. 
* **Licorice root**: May help with irritation and inflammation by protecting the gut lining. 
* **Slippery elm**: May help heal the mucous membranes in the gut. 
* **Gasha-jinki-gan**: A Japanese blend of 10 herbs that may help with fecal incontinence by reducing the urge to pass stool.
