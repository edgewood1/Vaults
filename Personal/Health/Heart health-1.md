# Heart health

Elaboration: 
* <u>**[Stress Tests:](https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&hl=en-us&cs=1&sca_esv=f873516cb1d87b20&q=Stress+Tests&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwixpryQ5PmMAxWbgIQIHXjxD9gQ4eYNegQIFBAD&mstk=AUtExfAk5WbMpQeeE5pPbbiZqsQfYn0VnIKOmAGe7s5Q4rRo252d4r0zZvsjVNu-8xioBOwDnGxBuyXWhI1PAEJkWeZ9X6kQLgFViNmxD7e9Wqe78i4yVBuL98aXKMk3SO9ImSk&csui=3)**</u>These tests can reveal blockages in heart arteries by monitoring your heart's activity while you exercise. 
* <u>**[Echocardiograms](https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&hl=en-us&cs=1&sca_esv=f873516cb1d87b20&q=Echocardiograms&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwixpryQ5PmMAxWbgIQIHXjxD9gQxccNegQIDhAB&mstk=AUtExfAk5WbMpQeeE5pPbbiZqsQfYn0VnIKOmAGe7s5Q4rRo252d4r0zZvsjVNu-8xioBOwDnGxBuyXWhI1PAEJkWeZ9X6kQLgFViNmxD7e9Wqe78i4yVBuL98aXKMk3SO9ImSk&csui=3)**</u>**:**These non-invasive scans use sound waves to create detailed images of the heart's structure and function. 
* <u>**[Cardiac CT Scans](https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&hl=en-us&cs=1&sca_esv=f873516cb1d87b20&q=Cardiac+CT+Scans&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwixpryQ5PmMAxWbgIQIHXjxD9gQxccNegQIDxAB&mstk=AUtExfAk5WbMpQeeE5pPbbiZqsQfYn0VnIKOmAGe7s5Q4rRo252d4r0zZvsjVNu-8xioBOwDnGxBuyXWhI1PAEJkWeZ9X6kQLgFViNmxD7e9Wqe78i4yVBuL98aXKMk3SO9ImSk&csui=3)**</u>**:**These scans can show blockages in the heart arteries. 
* <u>**[Cardiac MRI](https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&hl=en-us&cs=1&sca_esv=f873516cb1d87b20&q=Cardiac+MRI&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwixpryQ5PmMAxWbgIQIHXjxD9gQxccNegQIEBAB&mstk=AUtExfAk5WbMpQeeE5pPbbiZqsQfYn0VnIKOmAGe7s5Q4rRo252d4r0zZvsjVNu-8xioBOwDnGxBuyXWhI1PAEJkWeZ9X6kQLgFViNmxD7e9Wqe78i4yVBuL98aXKMk3SO9ImSk&csui=3)**</u>**s:**These scans use magnetic fields and radio waves to create detailed images of the heart. 
* <u>**[Holter Monitoring](https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&hl=en-us&cs=1&sca_esv=f873516cb1d87b20&q=Holter+Monitoring&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwixpryQ5PmMAxWbgIQIHXjxD9gQxccNegQIDRAB&mstk=AUtExfAk5WbMpQeeE5pPbbiZqsQfYn0VnIKOmAGe7s5Q4rRo252d4r0zZvsjVNu-8xioBOwDnGxBuyXWhI1PAEJkWeZ9X6kQLgFViNmxD7e9Wqe78i4yVBuL98aXKMk3SO9ImSk&csui=3)**</u>**:**A portable EKG device that records heart activity for 24 hours or more, potentially revealing intermittent heart rhythm issues that a standard EKG might miss.
