# Violin — Lesson Notes

## Basic setup & posture

- Scroll pointing up
- Fingers apart, stretch
- R hand: first finger not pointing; hand C-shape, pinky primary; thumb tip on clip
- Chin holds the violin
- Raise scroll up
- Sticker stays close to string
- Adjust yellow

## Bow technique

- Up/down with yr bow — each section begins and ends with an up
- Heavy vs light bow
- More heavy less; more long less
- Marshmallow hand
- Start and stop on stick (up/down)
- C-tunnel
- Sticker goes down on Allegro

## Bow strokes

- **Staccato**: stop in between
- **Legato (Allegato)**: connected, extended
- **Martelé (Hammered)**: strong, articulated stroke — sharp, percussive start; "hammered" due to pressure before release

## Scales

**G scale:** H2 b H2 F# L2 C L2 G
- Game: I sing whole row and she guesses; watch sticker

**D scale:** high 2s
