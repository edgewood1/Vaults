# Fina — Scripts & Conversation Guides

## Script: talking about a difficult moment (gift incident)

*For a calm moment — bedtime or quiet time together*

"Hey sweetie, can we talk for a minute about what happened with your new gift the other day? I've been thinking about it."

*(Pause, give her a chance to look up or nod.)*

"I got upset when you were angry and took it out on your gift. I told you we'd take a break from gifts for a while, and I want you to know why I said that — not to punish you, but because I want us both to learn how to handle big feelings without hurting things we love."

---

## Script: general template (gentle check-in)

*Written for a calm moment, maybe bedtime or quiet time together. Adapt as needed.*

"Hey sweetie, can we talk for a minute about what happened with you [the other day / at school / this morning]?"

*(Pause and give her space to respond.)*

"I've been thinking about it, and I want to understand how you felt..."
