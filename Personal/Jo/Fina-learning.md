## Art & STEM

Art is not entertainment — it's a way of cultivating intelligence.

STEM forces left-brain thinking. Art forces right or whole-brain thinking, which creates greater emotional intelligence and more holistic STEM thinking. "Product thinking" is the result of STEM only.

## Poison ivy

**3 ways to get it:**
1. Touch it directly (root, stem, berries, leaves)
2. Touch it indirectly — oil transfers via objects (ball, dog) that touched the plant
3. Burn poison ivy and inhale the smoke

**What causes the reaction:** the oil/urushiol on all parts of the plant

**Identifying poison ivy:**
- Three-leaf leaflets (most recognizable)
- Longer middle leaflet
- Small, round berries — start green, mature to white
- Hairy-rooted vine
