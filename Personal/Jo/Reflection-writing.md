Reflect

Reflection Questions for the Letter

1.	"What was the story of this morning?"

2.	"When you felt the 'Big Grump' coming on, what did your body feel like?"

3.	"What was the one thing you were most afraid of losing or missing out on this morning?"

4.	"After you stayed in your room and got dressed by yourself, how did your heart feel differently than when you were shouting?"


5.	"What is one thing we could try tomorrow to keep our 'Family Peace' safe, even if things don't go exactly as you expect?"
