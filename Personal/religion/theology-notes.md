# Theology Notes

## God and existence

God is one thing analogous — he is existence, which is good, true, beautiful, just, etc. However, God is not a thing or a category.

East and West seem to be saying the same thing but using different words. God's energy sounds like God's knowledge — described as the infinite ways one can relate to the infinite nature of existence.

Reading a description of the universe and seeing images of it helps. Then: knowledge that this is merely one dimension in a host.

*Have copies of: Philokalia, Lossky.*

## Human vs divine faculties

For humans:
- Identity / Knowing / Willing / Love
- Identity gets in the way
- Love is optional (conditioned)

For the divine:
- Knowing, willing, and love are all one

## Scripture and tradition
