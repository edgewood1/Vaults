## Contributions — 401k types

- **Pre-tax / Traditional:** deducted before taxes; $31K limit; taxed on withdrawal
- **Roth 401k:** deducted after taxes; $31K limit; NOT taxed after age 59.5
- **After-tax:** deducted after income tax withheld

## IRA rollover — potential downsides

- Loss of 401k loan options (IRAs don't allow borrowing)
- Reduced creditor protection (401ks have stronger federal protection)
