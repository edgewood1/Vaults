**HSA vs FSA:**
- HSA: you own it forever; funds never expire; triple tax advantage
- FSA: use-it-or-lose-it (limited rollover); employer-tied
- HSA as long-term strategy: invest the balance, pay current expenses out-of-pocket, let it compound
