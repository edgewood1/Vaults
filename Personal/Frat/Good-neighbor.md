## About the Program

The Duke Good Neighbor Program aims to foster positive relationships between Duke University and the surrounding Durham community. Collaborative effort involving Duke Student Affairs, Community Affairs, Duke student government, and Durham community organizations.

- Works with specific Durham neighborhoods — initially those closest to Duke's East and West campuses, expanded to include others
- Treats neighbor emails like police reports — but having a police report is a helpful additional tool in the student conduct process
- Generally follows up with students who live at each address and brings them into the student conduct process
- Can follow up with the property owner or management company as a partner

Program built on the Duke-Durham Neighborhood Partnership, established 1996.
