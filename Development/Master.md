⌨️ KEY SLASH COMMANDS (How to talk)

- /contract "[goal]"

- Triggers: The Architect persona.
- Action: Researching the codebase and drafting the prompt.md.

- /plan

- Triggers: The Plan-Agent persona.
- Action: Turning an approved Contract into a task-based plan.md.

- /compact

- Triggers: The Compactor persona.
- Action: Cleaning up handoff.md to save tokens/memory.

- /review

- Triggers: The Reviewer persona.
- Action: Checking code against the Contract and drafting the PR.

🚦 THE "CHECKPOINT" PROTOCOL (When to stop)

Stop and provide manual approval at these gates:

1. Gate A (The Contract): Is the prompt.md accurate? Does it have the right "Why"?
2. Gate B (The Blueprint): Are the steps in plan.md logical? Did the AI miss a file?
3. Gate C (The Pivot): If a task fails 3 times, stop the agent. Re-read handoff.md and manually edit the plan.md to provide a new path.

📝 THE "SOVEREIGN" RULES (How to think)

1. Context is King: If the AI is confused, the handoff.md is messy. Fix the file, fix the AI.
2. Altitude Matters: Humans handle Strategy (the what). AI handles Tactics (the how).
3. Promotion is Progress: Every bug fix is a lesson. Use the Reviewer to "promote" lessons into CLAUDE.md.
4. No Vibe Coding: Never start a task without a plan.md checkbox.

📓 AGENT PERSONALITY CHEAT SHEET

- The Architect (Strategist): High-level, critical, focused on the big picture.

- Motto: "Measure twice, cut once."

- The Reviewer (Skeptic): Pessimistic, detail-oriented, looks for bugs and edge cases.

- Motto: "Show me the evidence."

- The Implementer (Workhorse): Fast, iterative, focused on checking off plan.md.

- Motto: "Get it done."
