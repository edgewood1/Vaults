# Resume themes

* **Problem-solving** – [Many problems](https://hackernoon.com/10-soft-skills-every-developer-needs-66f0cdcfd3f7) are detected during the software development life cycle process. The developer should know how to identify and analyze the problem, and come up with possible solutions for it. He or she and the development team will then select which solution is the best.
* **Critical thinking** – This is assessing the facts and details of a certain problem before deciding and executing the best solution. [Critical thinking](https://jaxenter.com/6-most-useful-soft-skills-for-software-developers-125286.html) allows a developer to mentally test a solution before selecting which is the most efficient.
* **Adaptability** – This refers to a developer’s [openness to learn](https://jaxenter.com/6-most-useful-soft-skills-for-software-developers-125286.html) new skills and acquire additional responsibilities. Adaptability allows a developer to adjust to changing situations and react positively to comments and criticism about the results of his or her deliverables.
* **Empathy** – This allows developers to fully understand the problems that their team is facing and helps them work together to find solutions. Additionally, [empathizing](https://hackernoon.com/10-soft-skills-every-developer-needs-66f0cdcfd3f7) with the software’s intended users enables the developers to see things from their point of view so they can understand what they require from software being developed.
* **Continuous learning** – [Learning is a continuous process](https://jaxenter.com/6-most-useful-soft-skills-for-software-developers-125286.html). As the developers transfer from one company to another, they should continue to improve their skills and knowledge. They can also acquire additional knowledge through guidance from more experienced peers.
