# Ride share


**Community & Non-Profit Options:**
* **Cumberland County CTP:** Offers low-cost, curb-to-curb shared van service for seniors and disabled residents who lack transportation, with fares as low as $2.50 one-way for older adults. [Policies sheet](https://www.cumberlandcountync.gov/docs/default-source/community-transportation-documents/ada/ada-english/policies-and-procedures-(elderly-andor-disabled).pdf?sfvrsn=c4a9cc21_2) (M-F 9a-12p, request via phone 24 hrs ahead, be ready an hour before). Call to set up eligibility:  (910) 678-7619
* **FAST TRAC-ADA:** The city's paratransit service for those with disabilities, providing accessible rides for various needs in

**To get started, contact:**
* **Cumberland County CTP:** Visit their website or call for details on eligibility and scheduling.
* **NC Division of Aging:** Provides links to local providers and information. 

**What to ask:Medicaid:** Covers rides to Medicaid-covered medical appointments.
